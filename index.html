About

I live and breathe for digital creativity and what could be better than doing so in a world that is getting more and more digital as we speak. I love it.

My background spans almost 10 years now with experience from Web, Print, Television, Infomercials and consider myself a mobile expert with both a degree in Web development with focus on mobile platforms and extended knowledge about what works and what doesn't in our so ever expanding mobile world.

Not playing any favorites here (I love all creative things) but animation has always been very close to my heart and is something I consider my absolute biggest passion. Above in the projects section, you will see some proof of that.

About this page

This portfolio is done with a mobile first philosophy and built in pure non-bootstrapped HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript with help from external libraries such as jQuery, Greensock and OnScreen();. I have made it public for anyone to see/fork/clone/pull here at GitHub. Sharing is caring.
