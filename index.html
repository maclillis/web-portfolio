About

I live and breathe for digital creativity and what could be better than doing so in a world that is getting more and more digital as we speak. I love it.

My professional background spans almost 10 years now with experience from Web, Print, Television, Infomercials and consider myself a mobile expert with both a degree in Web development with focus on mobile platforms and extended knowledge about what works and what doesn't in our so ever expanding mobile world. I also have a huge interest in User Experience and always put user friendliness first in anything I do. I still have lot to learn but feel that I have good understanding of already.

Not playing any favorites here (I love all creative things) but animation has always been very close to my heart and is something I consider my absolute biggest passion. Above in the projects section, you will see some proof of that.

About this Portfolio

This portfolio is done with a mobile first philosophy and built in pure non-bootstrapped HTML5, CSS3 with some {LESS} and JavaScript with help from external libraries such as jQuery, Greensock and OnScreen();. I have made it public for anyone to see/fork/clone/pull here at GitHub. Sharing is caring.
